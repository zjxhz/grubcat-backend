FAQ
What is Grubwithus?

Grubwithus is a social dining network that brings individuals together at great restaurants. Meet new people, maintain old friendships and have fun social meal experiences!

How does it work?

You book individual reservations for the meals you want to attend and prepay online. The meal price, tax and gratuity are included, so you just show up to the meal, have a great dinner, and leave with new friends! Any drinks you order at the restaurant will be separately paid by you at the end of the meal.

What happens if less than 4 people reserve a meal?

Your credit card is not charged until 4 or more people reserve the meal. If, on the day of the meal, less than 4 people have reserved, the meal will be cancelled.

Why does the meal price increase per reservation?

We reward you for booking early! The meal price increases as demand increases.

Are there refunds?

Like any sporting event or concert, all sales are final. You can, however, transfer your ticket to anyone at anytime.

What about dietary restrictions?

Each restaurant tries to make its menu as veggie friendly as possible, but if you have dietary restrictions, please check the meal's menu before reserving.

How are meals created?

Most meals are created by our users or dining groups!

Why do some meals have restaurants that aren't visible?

Our main goal is to build and maintain friendships, with food being a very tasty benefit. You'll always know the restaurant's neighborhood, but to prevent restaurant cancellations, we usually add a partner restaurant only when the meal tips. Rest assured that you'll always enjoy a good meal with new friends!
